Who am I?

Tiger woods is a professional golfer that is tied for the most wins in pga tour history. Woods was born on December 30, 1975 and is 46 years old. Woods started his professional golf career at age 20. During his time on the PGA tour Woods had 82 wins. 15 of those wins were majors. Woods won the U.S. open four times, the PGA championship four times, the open championship three times and the masters five times.

My professional history

At age 20 in 1996 Woods dropped out of Standford University and started his professional golf career. After his first year of professional golf Woods had three wins on the PGA tour and became the best ranked golfer in the world by June 1997. Woods was ranked as the best golfer in the world during 1999-2004. Woods lost this ranking due to personal problems and did not regain it till 2013. Overall Woods has 82 PGA wins with 15 of those wins being majors.
